Delete Account Request

We are sorry to see you go. Requesting the deletion of your Dragon's Count account will permanently purge your data.

Account Deletion & Data Retention
  • Your profile details (email, display name, and avatar setting) are permanently and irreversibly purged.
  • Your active authorization sessions and security credentials are immediately revoked.
  • Cooperative Game History: Games you participated in remain active so other players don't lose their scores. However, your profile will be completely anonymized inside those games (your name displays as "Deleted" and avatar changes to a ghost 👻).
  • If you created any games and want them completely deleted, please delete them inside the app before initiating account deletion.
How to request account deletion

Click the button below to draft a pre-populated deletion request email. Please make sure to send the email from the account registered with your Dragon's Count profile.
